Software as a Service (SaaS)

What is Software as a service?(Salesforce): Software as a service allows companies to adopt cutting edge software applications to their businesses while also not having to deal with the disadvantages that comes with it such as maintaining complex hardware and software locally.

How does this work? SaaS works by being accessed over the internet. The software will be stored on the providers servers while the clients may access the applications through a web browser.

Platform as a Sevice (PaaS)

What is Platform as a service? Platform as a service is typically an operating system or programming language platform which allows users to create programming projects online while not having to store any of the code locally, instead saving it online in the cloud.

What are the advantages to PaaS? PaaS holds many advantages; the main ones being that it provides access to a range of coding languages on its platform to people who may not have access on their own hardware. Another advantage is that it allows users to access their coding projects on any device as long as they have internet access.

Infastructure as a Service (IaaS)

What is Infastructure as a Service? Infastructure as a service allows cloud providers to host infastructure components usually present on a local data center. For example cloud providers can offer specific components which clients can build their own IT systems with.

What are the advantages of IaaS? IaaS offers many advantages, the main one being that the hardware side of the system is managed by the cloud providers, saving their clients the stress and cost of creating and maintaining their own hardware systems.

Communication as a Service (CaaS)

What is Communication as a Service? Communication as a service is an umbrella term which covers many forms of communication which are available to companies. This includes Email, Voice over Internet Protocol(VoIP) and video conferencing services.

What are the advantages of CaaS? CaaS allows companies to pursue cheaper alternatives to installed software programmes therefore saving overall costs, CaaS also may increase efficiency of business processes which include meetings or conferences. CaaS allows multinational companies to schedule meetings and calls between various branches around the world, ensuring each sector of the company may stay in touch and co-ordinate no matter the distances.
